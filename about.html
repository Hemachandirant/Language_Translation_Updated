WiLexi

WiLexi is an advanced document translation tool designed to make multilingual communication seamless and efficient. Our mission is to bridge language gaps by providing accurate, secure, and easy-to-use translation services.

Translate a Document

Upload your documents and get instant, accurate translations while maintaining the original format and structure. Effortlessly translate your documents into multiple languages while preserving the original formatting. Our advanced translation engine supports a wide variety of file formats, ensuring that your text, images, tables, and other elements remain intact. Simply upload your document, choose the desired language, and let our system handle the rest. Whether you need to translate contracts, reports, or any other type of document, our tool provides accurate and culturally sensitive translations tailored to your specific needs.

Summarize a Document

Quickly generate concise summaries of lengthy documents with just a few clicks. Our summarization tool extracts key points and critical information, presenting them in a digestible format. This feature is perfect for busy professionals who need to understand the essence of a document without reading it in its entirety. Whether you're dealing with research papers, legal documents, or business reports, our tool helps you save time and stay informed.

View Your Documents

Organize and access all your uploaded and translated documents from a centralized location. Our 'My Documents' feature provides a user-friendly interface where you can view, edit, and manage your files. Star important documents for quick access, sort files by date, language, or file type, and easily retrieve past translations. This feature ensures that your documents are always at your fingertips, making it easier to collaborate, review, and share with others.

Preview

Get a quick glance at your translated documents before finalizing them. The preview feature allows you to review the translated content side by side with the original text, ensuring accuracy and consistency. This tool also lets you see how the translated document will look in its final format, giving you the opportunity to make any necessary adjustments before saving or sharing the file. It's a vital step to ensure that your translations meet your quality standards.
